Healthy Eating

Sometimes trying to eat healthy is next to impossible, due to the hectic nature of classes and other priorities that a student may need to balance in their lives. It can be incredibly easy to just go to the closest junk food around or go buy fast food, since its easy and "cheap". Except it's not; fast food gets pricey, and you may soon find yourself either without much cash, or not living a healthy lifestyle. We have a few recipe ideas that you can try, however; they're delicious and wonderfully cheap:
