Social

Want a social life but there are too many assignments due? or you have a job on top of that? Here are some ways to keep your good grades without sacrificing your social life

  • Manage your time! not many of students realize it, but a lot of their free time is spent wasted on things like Netflix (yes we all love it but it is a big distraction), social media, and even over-socializing (hanging on campus w/ friends can be a serious time killer too) and re-evaluate your time.
  • Look at what time you usually wake up and go to sleep, when do you usually study throughout the day, and from what you find make a simple schedule and on it reserve some times to study: an hour here or 30 minutes there, just to keep your brain sharp!
  • If all else fails use the 30 minutes rule! It's simple, after each class just take 30 minutes out of the day (or at the end of the day) to refresh your memory of the subject you just learned, do this a little bit everyday, and you are sure to keep that info in your noggin!
