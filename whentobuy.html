When To Buy Your Textbooks

Buying textbooks is probably the most annoying aspect of College, simply because you end up spending hundreds of dollars on something that you may never use. Some will wait until the first day of class to decided whether or not the textbook is needed, however you run the risk of needing it right away and it won't come in until weeks later. Also the price of textbooks is astonishing so you want to find the cheapest one you can and that involves shopping the used book section, because honestly do you really need a brand new Biology book that you are going to use once and never open again, especially if you are not a biology major.
