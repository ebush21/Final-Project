Savings

Saving can be tough, especially being a college student, but here are some easy go to tips and tricks to getting a bang for your buck no matter how you spend

  1. Always use coupons - not a lot of people ralize, but some of the best deals come when using coupons. You can find them in the Sunday paper, some places have online coupons that you can download, or you could
  2. Craiglslist is your friend- Craigslist is a one stop shop for any and everything you might need, like furniture, reliable used phones, even used cars!
    • Other Sites like this one include:
      • Ebay
      • Amazon
  3. Get Mint if you want your bank account looking fresh - Mint is a application that links to your bank accounts and helps you keep track of all your spending and even helps budget your money.
